AIR CONDITIONING

The cooperation between Mandilas Enterprises Limited and Carrier United Technologies Corporation is further testament to the ability of Mandilas to forge successful long term partnerships with internationally renowned brands. Mandilas became the Carrier Distributor in 1955 and is the oldest independent distributor in Africa


Our industry-leading security offerings include:

Engineering Sales: Mandilas air-conditioning expertise is made available to consultants, contractors and end users on major projects through its Engineering Sales Unit. We also distribute Carrier equipment and offers service on equipment design and selections.

Contracts: The Contracts Unit acts both to complement Engineering Sales and as a stand-alone contracting unit, in the installation of large central air conditioning systems throughout Nigeria. It also provides a fully computerized commissioning service on all central air conditioning systems.

Servicing: The Service unit provides 24/7 after-sales support (preventive & corrective) with professional equipment and experienced staff on the entire range of Carrier equipment i.e. window units, split units, packaged units VRF units and central air conditioning systems. We are also the main outlet in Nigeria for genuine Carrier spare parts for all types of equipment.

PROPERTIES

The Mandilas Head Office is a large, modern 16- storey office complex located at Mandilas House, 96/102, Broad Street, Lagos. The Mandilas Group owns this centrally located and attractive complex. This building is soon to be renovated to reflect the times. The company also owns the Mandilas Building, another modern 8-storey office building at 35, Simpson Street, Lagos, which currently serves as its Operations Headquarters. The Group has another 31 properties in the country which are either used for Group benefit or leased out

MOTORS

Mandilas Motors has been a household name in the Nigerian Automobile industry for close to 70 years.

The Volkswagen brand was introduced to Nigeria by the company in the ’50s; even before the establishment of the Volkswagen Assembly plant in Nigeria. Mandilas was later involved in the distribution of other brands like Audi,

Peugeot, Nissan, Daihatsu, Honda, Kia, and subsequently re-introduced the Skoda brand into Nigeria in the late ’90s. The company is currently one of the accredited Toyota dealers in Nigeria, and a major player at that, with many awards to show for it since 2003. The Leasing unit is involved in fleet management and vehicle leases.

BRAND NEW VEHICLES

Mandilas is a major player in the sales of new Toyota vehicles in Nigeria. All new vehicles sold are backed up with a three year or 100,000km (whichever comes first) Manufacturer’s warranty; with the option of a Service plan offered with every new vehicle sold. Various incentives are offered to our customers; and these include free registration, accessories, free service and much more based on terms and conditions of the sale

APPROVED USED VEHICLES

The department is responsible for procuring and selling of fairly used certified vehicles from individuals and corporate bodies. Used car vehicles undergo a twenty (21) point check to ensure that they are in a good working order. Vehicles are certified by a workshop team of competent technicians and are thereafter approved as certified fairly used cars for sale to the public. Mandilas offers a limited warranty on engine, differential and gearbox. Once again no other Toyota dealer in Nigeria has this option, and serves as proof the Mandilas is the leader in innovation and customer service
